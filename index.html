About our club

RMIT Techies Club (RTC), born in 2024 through the vision of Dao Duc Lam and Luong Chi Bach. We're a dynamic community driven by a shared passion for technology and innovation. From immersive coding sessions to thrilling hackathons, we offer a platform for students to learn, create, and thrive in this era of technology. Join us as we embark on a journey of exploration, collaboration, and endless possibilities!
